Case Study · Product Design · AdTech

Nativo
Widget Gallery

An ecosystem designed to bolster the capabilities of the Nativo platform

Introduction

Nativo is the most advanced platform for deploying, serving and optimizing integrated ads and brand content. Their goal is to incorporate content in the most engaging and educational way possible — requiring users to input content as seamlessly and efficiently as possible. The users responsible for this are campaign managers, content strategists, and the sales team, who need to incorporate widgets such as Google Maps, YouTube, Twitter, Skyscanner, and more.

Problem

Problem

  • Difficult to embed anything other than videos and images
  • Users are not familiar with coding
  • Implementing widgets with the current tools is time consuming
Goal

Goal

  • Reduce time spent on implementing widgets
  • Drop the involvement of software engineers aiding in widget implementation
  • A solution requiring no coding knowledge so campaign managers, content strategists, and the sales team could use it independently
Solution

Solution

We sought to create a widget ecosystem — private initially, with plans to open publicly in the future. The centerpiece would be the Widget Gallery: a marketplace to pick and choose widgets to place into content. We also built a Widget Editor tab system, allowing users to create their own widgets from scratch without any coding knowledge.

Results

The Widget Gallery simplified widget implementation for Nativo's users. The team can now deliver content for clients quickly and efficiently, with no developer involvement required.
